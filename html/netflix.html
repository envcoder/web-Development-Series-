What is the difference between Netflix and Hulu?

>

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of titles, including movies, TV shows, anime, documentaries, and more. Hulu, on the other hand, is a cable-TV service that offers a selection of movies, TV shows, and documentaries. Both services have their own unique features and offerings. Netflix is available worldwide while Hulu is available in the United States, Canada, and parts of Europe. Netflix offers a more diverse selection of titles and features, while Hulu focuses on providing a more popular and well-known content.
